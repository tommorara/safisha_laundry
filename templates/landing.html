👋 About the Project

Safisha Laundry Manager was created to solve a common pain point for small to mid-sized laundry business owners: manual and messy order tracking. With Safisha, owners can manage everything digitally—from receiving new orders to marking them as finished—all through a clean, intuitive interface.
